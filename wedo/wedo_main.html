"A to-do list app: WeDo"

"WeDo is a to-do list application"

Our group are trying to design a new generation To-Do list app, which allowed user sharing their tasks with friends. According to our research, doing tasks with each other will give more motivation. Also, as a To-DO list APP, WeDO provides a convient and easy way to build your own schedule.

"Social Media Platform

An interesting Social Media Platform based on the To-Do list, find more friends and join the task groups. Make and easier way to share and do tasks.

"Our Goals"

Our goal is that when users see our application, they can have a brignt geeling, be attracted by it and use it in depth. Letting users feel relaxed and happy os also one of our main goals, without spending a lot of time learning how to use WeDo. Finally, we hope to provide a benigh social platform through which users acan improve themselves and enrich their lives,
